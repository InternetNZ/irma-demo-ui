InternetNZ IRMA Demos

Kia ora!

This website brings some use cases that InternetNZ has prepared to present IRMA.

InternetNZ membership

Simulates InternetNZ membership purchase. As a reference of the trust chain on credentials already issued you will be asked to provide e-mail address and full name.

You may issue your basic credentials at simple issuer page.

Voting Card

It issues Voting Cards with anonymous token that can be use for voting purposes. It contains Election Title, Start Date and End Date.

Cake election

Simulates a election use case. Using an anonymous unique token from your Voting Card, you will be allowed to submit your vote. Only once.

You may issue your voting card at Voting Card. The election title must Cake Election.

Event Access Pass

Simulates an event pass acquisition with optional disclose of IRMA credentials. You can purchase the pass entirely by filling the form. Or you can use your InternetNZ membership to get a discount.

You may issue your membership at InternetNZ Membership.

Wine shop

During a Wine Shop, we check if the user has an attribute of age limit as a proof that the user is over 18. The user can also opt if he wants to buy as a guest or to sign a subscription. As a guest buyer, an full address will be asked, as a subscriber, name and address will be asked. On both cases, the user can fill manually or fill with IRMA attributes.

Age limits, address and name may be issued at simple issuer page.

IDNZ Card

Issuance of IDNZ Card, used to as a proof age limits. We simulate a login into a user portal to generate the card, that has its digital version valid for a month and holds name, birthdate, over18, over20 and over65 age limit.

With the IDNZ Card credential you can also test sign a consent of attributes sharing and confirm that the consent was signed by your IDNZ card number.

Name and email may be issued at simple issuer page.

Simple issuer

Simple form to issue basic attributes, such as full name, full address, e-mail, age limits, etc.

Simple verifier

Simple verifier of basic attributes.
